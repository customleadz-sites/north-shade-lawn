Landscaping & Design

Landscapes designed with you — built to last.

Sod & turf installation, garden beds, grading, landscape lighting, full landscape renovations and more — we design and install it all across Greenville, Edmore and the surrounding mid-Michigan area.

Overview

Design and build, done right.

Great landscaping is equal parts vision and groundwork. We start by walking your property and listening to how you want to use it, then design a layout — beds, plantings, grading and focal points — that fits your home and the way you live.

From there our crew handles the full install: shaping beds, prepping soil, planting, laying sod and finishing every edge. We choose plants suited to Michigan's seasons and your maintenance style, so your landscape looks intentional the day we leave and keeps getting better as it fills in.

New build? We handle new-construction landscaping start to finish, turning bare lots into finished, move-in-ready grounds.
