Is Landscape Lighting Worth It?

Homeowners often ask whether landscape lighting is worth it, or if it's just a nice-to-have. Our take after installing a lot of it: well-designed outdoor landscape lighting is one of the highest-return, most-noticed upgrades you can add to a property. It pays you back every single night — in curb appeal, safety, security and resale value.

Curb appeal that works after dark

You spent good money on your landscaping, hardscaping and home's facade — but for half the year in Michigan, it's dark by dinnertime and nobody can see any of it. Landscape lighting brings your trees, garden beds, walkways and stonework to life at night. It's the difference between a house that disappears at dusk and one that looks like a magazine cover.

Landscape lighting is the only upgrade that makes your whole property look better the moment the sun goes down — every night, all year.

Safety and security

Beyond looks, outdoor lighting earns its keep. Lit walkways, steps and driveways mean no more tripping in the dark. And a well-lit property is far less inviting to anyone who'd rather not be seen — landscape lighting is a genuine, low-key security upgrade for your home.

More usable outdoor space

Lighting your patio, deck and garden extends how long you actually use those spaces. Evenings on a well-lit paver patio feel like an outdoor room instead of a dark backyard. If you've invested in hardscaping, lighting is what lets you enjoy it after dark.

What about cost and upkeep?

Modern low-voltage LED landscape lighting sips electricity and the fixtures last for years, so running costs are minimal. It's a one-time install that keeps paying off — and it's known to boost a home's perceived value and resale appeal, which makes the answer to "is it worth it?" an easy yes.

  • Highlights landscaping, trees and hardscaping at night
  • Safer walkways, steps and driveways
  • Added security around the home
  • Energy-efficient LED — low running cost
  • Boosts curb appeal and resale value
