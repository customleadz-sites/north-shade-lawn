Our story

Local roots. High standards.

North Shade Lawn was built on a simple idea: do great work, treat people right, and stand behind everything you put in the ground.

Who we are

A mid-Michigan company that treats your property like its own.

For four years, North Shade Lawn has helped homeowners and businesses across the Greenville and Edmore area build, plant and maintain grounds they're proud of.

We're a locally owned landscaping and hardscaping company — not a faceless franchise. When you hire us, you get a crew that knows your property and owners who answer the phone. We focus on the work most companies rush: clean bed edges, level pavers, healthy plants, and a finished site that's actually finished.

Most of our landscaping and hardscaping is residential, while our weekly lawn care and seasonal snow contracts keep commercial properties and HOAs running smoothly all year. Whatever the job, the standard is the same.
